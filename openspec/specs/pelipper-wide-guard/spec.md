## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Pelipper Moveset and Wide Guard Integration
Pelipper SHALL have Wide Guard and Scald in its competitive move set, replacing Protection.

#### Scenario: Blocking Spread Moves
- **WHEN** the opponent uses a spread move (e.g. Earthquake, Heat Wave, Eruption, Rock Slide)
- **THEN** Pelipper can use Wide Guard to completely protect both itself and its ally (e.g. Blastoise) from all spread damage that turn

#### Scenario: Preserving Rain STAB
- **WHEN** Pelipper needs to attack with a Water move under Rain
- **THEN** Pelipper can use Scald (Escaldar) to deal direct Water-type damage and potentially burn the target, complementing Blastoise's attacks.
