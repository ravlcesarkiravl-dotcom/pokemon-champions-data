## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Blastoise Dark Pulse Integration
Blastoise SHALL replace Ice Beam with Dark Pulse in its competitive move set.

#### Scenario: Encountering Ghost Types
- **WHEN** Blastoise faces a Ghost-type Pokemon (like Sinistcha or Gengar)
- **THEN** Blastoise can use Dark Pulse to deal 120 BP (boosted by Mega Launcher) x2 super-effective damage

#### Scenario: Coverage Against Ice Weakness
- **WHEN** Blastoise faces a Dragon-type Pokemon
- **THEN** the team will rely on Mamoswine's Ice moves or Blastoise's massive neutral Water damage
