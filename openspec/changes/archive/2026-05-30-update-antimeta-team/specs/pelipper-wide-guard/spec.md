## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Pelipper Wide Guard Integration
Pelipper SHALL replace Hydro Pump with Wide Guard in its competitive move set.

#### Scenario: Blocking Spread Moves
- **WHEN** the opponent uses a spread move (e.g. Earthquake, Heat Wave, Eruption, Rock Slide)
- **THEN** Pelipper can use Wide Guard to completely protect both itself and its ally (e.g. Blastoise) from all spread damage that turn

#### Scenario: Preserving Rain STAB
- **WHEN** Pelipper needs to attack with a Water move
- **THEN** the team will rely on Blastoise's Water Spout in rain, or Pelipper's Hurricane, mitigating the loss of Hydro Pump
